Is studying in Paris libraries worth the hype?

Paris is renowned for its historical libraries, and the Bibliothèque nationale de France is often cited as one of the most inspiring places to study.

During my visit to the Salle Ovale at BnF Richelieu, I wanted to understand whether the space truly enhances focus or whether the hype outweighs the experience.

I spent several hours working on mathematics and computer science coursework, surrounded by students immersed in their own disciplines.
